Hi, I'm Dusan, an overall creative person, with a good eye for design, and a technical skill-set for making pretty websites.

My passion are stunning visuals and I really enjoy creating them. I have studied arts and design and worked for a decade as a freelance costume and multimedia designer before I dedicated myself to web development.
Using experiences collected in the other spheres of design and applying them to the websites that I am creating, gives my projects a distinctive look and feel.
Just like a stage costume, a website needs to be custom tailored to fit the needs and intentions of the client, but also to tell a story using visual cues. So I do like to treat a website like a digital piece of art.
My personal interest in distinctive design draws me towards the projects that require memorable online presentations and unique visual solutions. So if you are interested in working with me, please let's get in touch and see what I can do for you.
